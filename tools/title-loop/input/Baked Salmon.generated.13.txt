BAKED SALMON

- 2 tbsp sugar
- Salt to taste
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tbsp sugar

Directions
1. Plate and serve
2. Cook for 20 minutes
3. Preheat oven
4. Garnish with fresh basil
5. Mix ingredients together

Servings: 4
