PASTA CARBONARA

INGREDIENTS:
- Salt to taste
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 100g butter
- Salt to taste

Instructions
1. Plate and serve
2. Preheat oven
3. Cook for 20 minutes
4. Plate and serve
5. Mix ingredients together
6. Plate and serve
7. Cook for 20 minutes
8. Plate and serve

Servings: 4
