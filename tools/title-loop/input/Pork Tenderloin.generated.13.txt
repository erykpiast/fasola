PORK TENDERLOIN

Ingredients
- Salt to taste
- 1001 butter
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tbsp sugar

Instructions
1. Mix ingredients together
2. Preheat oven
3. Cook for 20 minutes
4. Add seasonings
5. Garnish with fresh basil
6. Preheat oven
7. Add seasonings

Tip: can be prepared the day before
