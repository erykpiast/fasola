RED VELVET CAKE

Ingredients
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup milk
- 100g butter
- Salt to taste
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 100g butter

Directions
1. Plate and serve
2. Cook for 20 minutes
3. Add seasonings
4. Plate and serve
5. Garnish with fresh basil
6. Preheat oven
7. Add seasonings

Servings: 4
